How this protects you

Without a WebAuthn assertion the Mastio can only trust the Connector to claim "user X is using me". An attacker who compromises the Connector workload could mint sessions for any local user. With WebAuthn enabled the Mastio verifies a fresh assertion produced by your authenticator (Touch ID, YubiKey, Windows Hello) at every session emission. The private key never leaves the authenticator.
